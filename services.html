Our Services

Rent Collection

Ensure a consistent positive cash flow by collecting rent from each tenenat on a monthly basis.

Financial Reporting

Offer transparent monthly reporting to landlords, providing a comprehensive understanding of the property's status.

Repair and Maintenance

Efficiently manage contractor work for repairs and maintenance. Proactively identify potential issues to save landlords money. Conduct thorough inspections to ensure work meets high standards and prevent future maintenance problems.

Tenant Relations

Serve as the primary point of contact for tenants, addressing their questions, concerns, and requests promptly and respectfully. Valuing tenant satisfaction and maintaining the property leads to long-term tenancy and increased profitability.

Tenant Move Out Inspections

Conduct thorough inspections of tenant spaces before their move-out to prevent conflicts. Clearly communicate the building's expectations to tenants before returning the space to the landlord.

Bill Payments

Streamline the process of bill payments by closely monitoring the building's cash flow and budgeting on a monthly basis. Ensure timely and accurate payment of bills while maintaining financial stability.
